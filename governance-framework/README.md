# AI Governance Framework

This document presents a structured enterprise AI governance and lifecycle oversight model informed by applied AI initiatives in regulated environments.

The framework synthesizes practical implementation experience into a repeatable, risk-tiered structure designed to guide responsible, transparent, and scalable AI adoption.

---

## Framework Scope

The governance model addresses the full AI lifecycle, including:

- AI use case qualification and risk classification  
- Data governance, representativeness, and bias assessment  
- Model validation, explainability, and performance transparency  
- Deployment readiness and operational controls  
- Continuous monitoring, drift detection, and auditability  
- Workforce impact, fairness, and ethical safeguards  

---

## Governance Objectives

The framework is designed to:

- Embed responsible AI principles at each lifecycle stage  
- Maintain human accountability in high-impact systems  
- Provide structured validation checkpoints prior to deployment  
- Support documentation and audit readiness  
- Reduce unintended bias and adverse impact risk
